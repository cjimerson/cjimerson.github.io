grief & healing

Loss is a part of life, and everyone will experience it. The loss may be anticipated, or it may hit your blind-side, and knock the wind out of you. Regardless of your experience, we all must grieve at some point in our lives. There is no one way to grieve, nor is there a time-limit on how long one person will. It’s emotionally taxing, and difficult. One may find it easier to suppress their feelings altogether. However, you must deal with your loss in order to move forward in life, and not anchor yourself in the past.

benefits of journaling

Writing is a form of emotional therapy. Whether it be for future reflection, or for closure, this portal provides a means of chronicling thoughts, and recording stories, by sending notes from the heart. It’s your personal space. To say whatever you need to say. However you want to say it.

let us help you

Grieving is both individual & collaborative. Through journaling and recording everyday experiences and archiving your thoughts, following the loss of a loved one. The service will help people connect user with others by sharing journal entries and stories via posts, and message boards, and offering live chats with volunteer ‘grief coaches.’
